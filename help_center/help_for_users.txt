To start up the system enter the "prisoner" tab on the Manu, in the prisoner`s device.
Then, enter the "Main" tab, to track the prisoner. Make sure to wait few seconds before checking the location at the first time.
Choose the prisoner on the list, then press "Submit".
The prisoner`s personal ID and current location will appear on the screen.
To add additional restricted areas to this prisoner, you may use the "add red circles" window.
All problems and alerts about prisoners will appear in the "Alert log" window.
On the left you can see the selected prisoner`s location in the map, and his circles.
To erase old prisoners, you may find the erasing option un the right button corner of the map.
For additional help you may assist the supervisor.
